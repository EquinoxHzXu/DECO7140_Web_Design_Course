EXAMVIEWCR - DIGITAL CR IMAGING SYSTEM

The ExamvueCR is a low-cost solution for converting from film to digital x-ray. The system includes a Computer loaded with acquisition software, CD Burning, and DICOM print along with software licenses for 2 additional viewing stations which if not needed can be used to reduce the final price further. System includes two 14x17" plates with options for additional plates in either 14x17 or 10x12*.

* = 10x12 plates are loaded into a 10x14 cassette to allow for easy insertion into the scanner which has a 14 inch opening.


Important To Note Features:

  • Higher Quality Images/No Rollers Touch the CR Plates:

  • Unlike most other CR scanning system, the ExamvueCR does not have rollers that contact the phosphorous plate in any way. The design of this scanner utilizes a straight-in feed path and foil backing to the plate. Internal components of the scanner only touch the foil backing and never come into contact with the CR plate itself. The #1 reason for CR image quality degeneration is dust that finds its way onto the scanners rollers, then impinge that dirt onto your expensive CR plates. That leaves artifacts you will see on every image!

  • System Includes Computer:

  • Comes complete with desktop computer and 21" LCD monitor, 500G Hard Drive, and CD Burner with installed image acquisition software to operate the scanner.

  • Wide Range of available scan speeds:

  • Available in 25, 40, 60 and 75 Plates Per Hour scan speeds with associated different price points.

  • Compact:

  • System can be wall-mounted (With optional wall mount bracket) to minimize counter space needed to house the scanner.

  • Free iOS/Android Viewer applications:

  • Viewer applications available for iOS and Android devices in the App Store under "Quantor View" or The Android Play Store under "QuantorTab".
